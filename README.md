# Penelope

> She runs the home while Odysseus is away.

**Penelope** is the operating system for a small business that runs itself — distilled to one Telegram chat.

You give it your business name, your channels, and your hours. It handles customer DMs across Facebook, SMS, email, and Instagram. It quotes jobs, books appointments, processes payments, asks for reviews, runs marketing, and pings you with a daily brief. You only see the parts that need you.

Built on the [Loom](https://github.com/Mushisushi28/loom) multi-agent platform and themed with the [Odysseus](https://github.com/Mushisushi28/odysseus) dashboard.

## Status

🌒 **Pre-alpha.** v0.1 ships tonight. Use at your own risk.

## Quick start (when ready)

```bash
npx penelope init
```

5 questions, 90 seconds. You're running.
